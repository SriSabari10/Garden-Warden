Spinach

Spinach is a superfood. It is loaded with tons of nutrients in a low-calorie package. Spinach has been used by various cultures throughout history, notably in Mediterranean, Middle-Eastern, and South-East-Asian cuisines. It can be incorporated quite easily into any diet, as it is cheap and easy to prepare.
